Breaking the Cycle of Poverty

Zaman empowers women to break the cycle of intergenerational poverty by providing mothers the support, training, and employment needed to lift their families out of poverty.

Our Mission

Zaman is committed to facilitating change and advancing the lives of marginalized women and children by enabling them to meet essential needs common to all humankind.
